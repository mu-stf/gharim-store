# Audio Narration Files

This folder contains the welcome narration audio for the Iraqi Islamic Marketplace.

## Required Files:

Place your audio narration files here:

- **narration-en.mp3** - English narration
- **narration-ar.mp3** - Arabic narration

## Audio Specifications:

- **Format**: MP3
- **Recommended Quality**: 128-192 kbps
- **Duration**: Keep it brief (30-60 seconds recommended)
- **Content**: Welcome message introducing the marketplace

## Example Narration Script:

### English (narration-en.mp3)
*"Welcome to the Iraqi Islamic Marketplace. We offer a curated selection of traditional products inspired by Iraqi culture and Islamic values. Explore our collection and experience authentic quality."*

### Arabic (narration-ar.mp3)
*"مرحباً بكم في السوق الإسلامي العراقي. نقدم مجموعة مختارة من المنتجات التقليدية المستوحاة من الثقافة العراقية والقيم الإسلامية. استكشف مجموعتنا واختبر الجودة الأصيلة."*

## Usage:

The audio player will automatically:
- Switch between languages when the user changes the language toggle
- Display a play/pause button on the homepage
- Show appropriate text in English/Arabic based on the selected language

---

**Note**: Until you add the audio files, the audio player will show but won't play anything. This is expected behavior.
